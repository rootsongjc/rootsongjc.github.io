Sky Ao

Sky Ao, senior coder, sixteen years of software development experience, microservices expert, Service Mesh evangelist, and co-founder of Servicemesher community. Focusing on infrastructure, Cloud Native advocates, agile practitioners, and architects who adhere to the development of front-line polishing craftsmanship.
